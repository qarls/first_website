Test Page about One Shot

  • Author: Qarls
  • Date: October 15, 2025

One Shot is an incredible visual story-telling game involving the adventures of Niko across a desolate dying world. The player will solve various puzzles, navigate the story, fulfill various quests in the objective of delivering the proclaimed Sun that will save the world from inevitable self-destruction.

Key elements include, but not limited to:

  • The World Machine
  • Niko
  • Prophetbot
  • Calamus and Alula!
  • The Sun

You can find the game on Steam at OneShot. I highly recommend playing. It's a really good game!
